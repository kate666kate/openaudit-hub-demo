Executive summary

What this site needs first

Top takeaway

Truck Hardware is in solid shape overall, but repeated template issues are dragging down accessibility and consistency. The biggest return comes from fixing shared product-page components once rather than editing pages one by one.

What to do next

Prioritize image alt text, control naming, and contrast cleanup. Those three themes account for most findings and are realistic to explain in an interview as content-plus-theme work.

Largest risk

Repeated template issues

Product-page patterns generate the same problems across multiple URLs.

Fastest win

Image alt text

A focused content and theme pass can remove up to 97 image-related findings.

Best interview line

Fix once, improve many pages

This sounds strategic and shows platform thinking instead of page-by-page patching.
